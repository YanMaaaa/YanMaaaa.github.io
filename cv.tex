\section{Research Interests}
    My research interests lie at the intersection of reinforcement learning and multimodal models. In 2025, I built substantial experience in RL for vision-language models, especially RL scaling across single-visual tasks, multi-visual tasks, and agentic vision tool use. Prior to this, I also worked on unified multimodal models and text generation. My current focus is pre-training for multimodal generative models.
