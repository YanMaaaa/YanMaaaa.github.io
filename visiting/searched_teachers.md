- Jiatao Gu，UPenn CIS。最贴你给的参考系：主页明确写了 multi-modal generative models、world modeling、

  之一。

- Kai-Wei Chang，UCLA CS。主页明确写 Multimodal Foundation Models；近两年组里有 LaViDa、OpenVLThinker
  这类 VLM/多模态 reasoning/RL 相关工作。很适合你想做 VLM + multimodal RL。
  Homepage (https://web.cs.ucla.edu/~kwchang/) | Recent VLM/RL paper
  (https://web.cs.ucla.edu/~kwchang/bibliography/deng2025openvlthinker/)
- Heng Ji，UIUC CS。官方简介直接写 Vision-Language Models、Knowledge-enhanced LLMs、Multimedia；更偏多
  模态理解、知识增强、reasoning，不是 world model 主线，但很强。
  UIUC faculty (https://siebelschool.illinois.edu/about/people/faculty/hengji) | Recent VLM-related no
  te (https://siebelschool.illinois.edu/about/people/all-faculty/hengji)
