## Robotics-Fit Caveat

Yan's current strongest profile is multimodal RL / VLM post-training, tool-use agents, video data foundations, and predictive multimodal learning. He does not currently have direct real-robot, control, hardware, tactile sensing, manipulation deployment, or sim-to-real experience.

So for robotics-adjacent professors, the pitch should be:

> upstream video/world-model/foundation-model work that can support embodied agents

not:

> real-robot manipulation, control, hardware deployment, or robot policy execution.

Robotics-risk labels used below:

- **Low robotics risk**: mainly video/world models/foundation models/agents; no need to present as a robotics student.
- **Medium robotics risk**: embodied/robotics applications appear, but a video/model/data project can still fit naturally.
- **High robotics risk**: group is strongly centered on real robots, manipulation, control, hardware, tactile sensing, or robot deployment. Only contact if the email is framed as upstream video/world-model work.
